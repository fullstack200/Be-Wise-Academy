Services Offered

Group Tutoring

Parents commonly express concerns about group tutoring, fearing that a consistent pace may disadvantage slower learners. To address this, we've effectively countered these worries by maintaining an optimal 8:1 student-to-teacher ratio, achieving outstanding results. This ratio not only ensures individualized attention, making the learning process more meaningful for students, but also sets us apart. Visit our center today, engage with our Academic Counselor, and discover firsthand our innovative approach to education.

Online 1 on 1 Tutoring

Unbelievably, we've mastered the art of online tutoring. Having clocked over 20,000 hours of virtual classes since 2018, we've become specialists in Cambridge IGCSE and IB curriculums. Beyond excelling in Math and Science, we've triumphed in delivering effective online tuition for subjects like English, Spanish, and French. With a team of the right tutors tailored for your child's needs, our charging system is based on an hourly rate. Reach out to our student counselor today for a deeper dive into what we offer.
